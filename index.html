Workouts

Pushups

Pushups strengthen your chest, shoulders, and triceps. Great for upper body strength.

Do 3 sets of 12 daily. Keep your core tight for maximum effect. Breathing is essential: inhale down, exhale up.

Situps

Situps target your core muscles, improving posture and stability.

Start with 3 sets of 15 reps. Gradually increase reps over time for better endurance.

Plank

Planks build core endurance, balance, and overall strength.

Hold for 30-60 seconds. Keep your body straight, and focus on your breathing. Add variations like side planks.

Lunges

Lunges strengthen legs and glutes, improve balance.

Do 3 sets of 10 reps per leg. Keep your back straight and step far enough for full range of motion.

Squats

Squats strengthen your legs, glutes, and core.

Perform 3 sets of 15 reps. Keep your knees aligned with your toes. Add weights for more intensity.

Burpees

Burpees are full-body exercises that build strength and endurance.

Do 3 sets of 10 reps. Keep a steady pace to avoid burnout. Focus on form over speed.

Mountain Climbers

Cardio and core workout in one move.

Perform for 30-45 seconds per set. Keep your core tight and back flat.

Jumping Jacks

Classic cardio exercise for stamina and warm-up.

Perform for 30-60 seconds. Keep arms fully extended for maximum effect.

Yoga

Flexibility, balance, and relaxation.

Practice daily or several times per week. Focus on breathing and posture.

Meditation

Improve mental health, focus, and relaxation.

Spend 5-15 minutes daily. Use guided sessions if needed.

Benefits of Exercises

Muscle Strength

Regular strength training builds muscles and increases metabolism.

Combine with proper nutrition for best results. Include protein-rich foods and stay hydrated.

Cardio Health

Cardio improves heart health, endurance, and overall fitness levels.

Include 20-30 minutes daily for optimal results. Choose running, cycling, or swimming.

Flexibility

Stretching and yoga improve range of motion and prevent injury.

Include 5-10 minutes of stretching post-workout for recovery.

Nutrition Tips

Protein Intake

Protein supports muscle repair and growth after workouts.

Include eggs, chicken, legumes, and dairy in your meals. Spread intake throughout the day.

Hydration

Drink enough water to support metabolism and recovery.

Aim for 2-3 liters daily. Increase if exercising heavily or in hot weather.
