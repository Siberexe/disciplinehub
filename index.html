Strength Through Discipline

Follow routines, exercise consistently, eat clean, and master your body and mind.

About

Pushups

Pushups strengthen chest, arms, shoulders and core.

Doing 20-50 per day improves endurance and stability.

Situps

Core exercise targeting abs and hip flexors.

Consistent practice enhances posture and balance.

Plank

Engages core, back, shoulders, and legs.

Start with 30s-1min daily to build stability.

Exercise Benefits

Muscle Strength

Regular resistance exercise boosts muscle mass and metabolism.

Cardio Health

Improves heart, lungs, and overall endurance.

Flexibility

Daily stretches reduce injuries and increase range of motion.

Nutrition Tips

Protein Intake

Essential for muscle recovery. Include eggs, lean meats, and legumes.

Fruits & Veggies

High in vitamins, minerals, and fiber. Eat 3-5 servings daily.

Hydration

Drinking enough water maintains energy, focus, and metabolism.
