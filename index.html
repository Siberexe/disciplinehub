● Pipeline

How It Works

📷

Camera Feed Captured

Your device's camera streams live video frames directly into the browser — no data ever leaves your device.

🧠

AI Holistic Model Runs

Google MediaPipe's Holistic model processes each frame locally using WASM + WebGL for GPU-accelerated inference.

📍

Landmarks Extracted

543+ normalized 3D coordinates are extracted per frame: face, two hands, and full body pose with visibility scores.

🎨

Visual Effects Rendered

A Canvas 2D layer paints neon skeletons, glowing meshes, particle trails and energy orbs on top of your video feed.

⚡

60fps Output Displayed

The final composited frame is shown on screen — silky smooth, fully private, running entirely client-side.
